Introduction

Md. Faheemul Haque Faheem

My journey in engineering began with a deep fascination for how structures shape our daily lives. From towering skyscrapers to intricate bridge designs, I've always been captivated by the perfect blend of mathematics, physics, and creativity that defines civil engineering.
